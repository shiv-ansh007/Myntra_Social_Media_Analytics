##  Key Insights

- Video content generates the highest average engagement.
- Platform performance varies based on content type.
- Higher ad spend does not always guarantee higher ROI.
- Some less frequently used hashtags outperform highly used ones.
- Campaign engagement uplift was observed during promotional periods.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Prioritize video-led campaigns for major launches.
- Optimize budget allocation based on engagement ROI.
- Use platform-specific content strategies.
- Refine hashtag strategy based on performance, not frequency.
- Scale high-performing campaigns and formats.
